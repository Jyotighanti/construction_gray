About Us

TECHNO FAB

We are proud to present ourselves as a leading manufacturer of Pre Engineered Buildings (PEB), Civil Construction Works, and EOT Cranes, enjoying the esteemed recognition as the largest producer of PEB structures in North Karnataka. Our state-of-the-art facility is located in the vibrant Gamangatti Industrial Area, Hubballi, Karnataka State.

Company Strength:

We come from a rich legacy of fabrication expertise, with over 25 years of experience in this industry. Our commitment to innovation ensures we stay updated with cutting-edge technology, empowering us to tackle all challenges related to PEB structures across a variety of applications.

We design, manufacture & carry out erection at site for simple to highly sophisticated projects in any part of India. We also undertake “TURNKEY” projects including civil works. In a nutshell, we provide to customers “END TO END SOLUTIONS”.

We extend our heartfelt appreciation to our valued customers for their unwavering support, which has allowed us to thrive with immense pride today.

Our solutions are closely aligned with our customers' aspirations. We conduct thorough research, design, and development to present a diverse range of tailored solutions, helping our clients select the most suitable options.
