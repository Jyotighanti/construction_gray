TECHNO FAB

We are proud to present ourselves as a leading manufacturer of Pre Engineered Buildings (PEB), Civil Construction Works, and EOT Cranes, enjoying the esteemed recognition as the largest producer of PEB structures in North Karnataka. Our state-of-the-art facility is located in the vibrant Gamangatti Industrial Area, Hubballi, Karnataka State.

Company Strength:

We come from a rich legacy of fabrication expertise, with over 25 years of experience in this industry. Our commitment to innovation ensures we stay updated with cutting-edge technology, empowering us to tackle all challenges related to PEB structures across a variety of applications.

We design, manufacture & carry out erection at site for simple to highly sophisticated projects in any part of India. We also undertake “TURNKEY” projects including civil works. In a nutshell, we provide to customers “END TO END SOLUTIONS”.

We extend our heartfelt appreciation to our valued customers for their unwavering support, which has allowed us to thrive with immense pride today.

Our solutions are closely aligned with our customers' aspirations. We conduct thorough research, design, and development to present a diverse range of tailored solutions, helping our clients select the most suitable options. Our firm is led by a technically qualified partner with over 35 years of business experience, ensuring excellence in every project we undertake.

Projects Scope at our Factory:

We specialize in a wide array of projects, including Factory Buildings, Shopping Malls, Cinema Theatres, Warehouses, Large Go-Downs, Cold Storage Units, Multi-Storied Complexes, and Roofing Solutions for Stadiums. Our capabilities extend to accommodating spaces ranging from 5,000 to an impressive 250,000 Sq.Ft., all under one roof.

Mission:

Our Ambition: To establish ourselves as the leading PEB manufacturer throughout South India by 2027. Company Registration: Proudly registered under MSME.

Considering the upcoming market demand for PEB Structures, Company has taken a lead role in the Design, Manufacture, Erection & Commissioning PEB structures since 2010.
